# Whitepaper: Federated Quantum AI

## Abstract

Federated Quantum AI represents the convergence of distributed machine learning and quantum computing, enabling collaborative model training across quantum and classical computing resources without centralized data aggregation. This whitepaper explores the theoretical foundations, architectural components, and implementation strategies for Federated Quantum AI systems that combine the computational advantages of quantum algorithms with the privacy-preserving properties of federated learning.

## 1. Introduction

The rapid advancement of quantum computing technologies and the increasing demand for privacy-preserving machine learning have created new opportunities for innovative distributed computing paradigms. Federated Quantum AI emerges as a solution that leverages quantum computational advantages while maintaining data privacy through federated learning principles. This approach enables organizations to collaboratively train quantum-enhanced models without sharing sensitive data, creating unprecedented opportunities for secure, collaborative artificial intelligence.
